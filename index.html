Our Story

Born from Fire & Tradition

At Crimson & Ember, we believe that cooking with fire is more than a technique; it is a primal connection to the essence of ingredients. Founded by award-winning Chef Marcus Thorne, our kitchen is a theatre of heat, smoke, and precision.

We source exclusively from sustainable local farms and international purveyors of rare spices, crafting a menu that is unapologetically bold, deeply satisfying, and visually striking.
